Who
we are

Queen Onyemaechi and Yitschaq Abia had the belief that other travelers would want to share the experience and authentic adventures of travel in an affordable and luxurious style.
With the original goal of traversing Africa, we now offer trips around our big beautiful world! While things may be changing around the world, our aim and core value is to create an easy, affordable and luxury travel experience with Safety, personal taste, and budget in mind.

Why Quyi Travels?

Like Minded Groups

Quyi travels offers its tours to people from all over, allowing you travel to experience cultures and people who have a world of different experiences, backgrounds, perspectives, and stories to tell. Together you can seek out new adventures, make lifelong friendships, and see new places all with other like-minded travelers.

Solo Travelers are welcome

If you’re traveling solo, you’ll fit right in. Age and travel experience are not important, because in Quyi you will be in good company. Our tour guide and partners will make sure your trip is full of local insights, adventure , and they will do everything they can to enrich your travels experience.

Keep Costs Down

Even if you are traveling solo, Quyi travels will help keep costs down by partnering you up with a roommate. But If you would like to have a room to yourself that’s also fine - Quyi can arrange this at an extra cost

Freedom To Explore

Quyi knows that when traveling, a sense of freedom and security is important. We make sure that all the important plans are in place to give your trip structure, direction, and safety, but allowing you the freedom to do as you wish while on your travels
